  1. Introduction

    2. The purpose of this project is to add one component, which is rent, return vehicles, exchange points to rent or buy vehicles, and buy insurance by customer, for a typical Car Rental company called SuperRent.

    Developer information:

    David: Network security, User Interface, Coding

    Ashu:

    Lili: student from CST bachelor program with extended knowledge about database.

    Company information:

    The main operation for the SuperRent is to rent cars and trucks to its customer. The company has multiple locations in various cities. In order to meet customers’ different demand, the company offers car rental with broad range of cars and trucks. The reward system also involves different discount for the company’s loyal customer.

    Project information:

    The project includes four components:

    • Customer membership, points system, reservations
    • Customer vehicle rental, return, point deduction, and insurance purchase
    • Assistance from clerk for customer vehicle rentals, return
    • Manager association for used vehicle sale, sales rates, cost, reports

    The component included in this project is the second part: customer vehicle rental, return, point deduction, and insurance purchase.

  2. Background

    3. Project description:

    This project is one of the components for the whole SuperRent system. The user is the customer. In this component, our software system mainly includes four activities: customer vehicle rental, return, point deduction, and insurance purchase:

    • Customer vehicle rental: after the customers provide their information, they can make a reservation and have the rental agreement with credit card payment information.
    • For the vehicle return, the customer need to provide their data, time, odometer reading and make sure the gas tank is full.
    • The reward system includes the rules about how to collect points and deduct points. When customers join the club, each individual can get 500 points. After the initial points, club members can gain 1 point for each $5 they spend. To deduct the point, the club members can exchange 1000 points for one day rental for a premium or lower-ranking car. Or, 1500 points for one day renal for a luxury car, SUV, van or truck.
    • The customer can decide whether they need to have insurance purchase for the whole rental period. The payment need to be made at the point that vehicle is returned.

    This component in our project will help the company collect the information about the vehicle rental, payment, point tracking, return and insurance purchase.
